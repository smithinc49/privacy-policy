Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 11, 2025  ·  Last Updated: June 11, 2025

Overview

This privacy policy describes how SMS messages are sent and received as part of the Smith Family Fitness automated wellness program. This is a private, personal-use system operated by Christopher Smith and used solely by Christopher Smith and Victoria Smith.

Who We Are

This service is operated by Christopher Smith, Kennett Square, Pennsylvania. It is not a commercial product and is not affiliated with any business entity. Contact: chris@brandywineoaktax.com

How SMS Is Used

Automated SMS messages are sent twice per week to two pre-enrolled recipients — Christopher Smith and Victoria Smith — containing personalized workout plans. Recipients may reply with workout results and receive an automated response. No other parties receive messages through this system.

Opt-In & Consent

Both recipients have provided direct personal consent to receive these messages. No third-party opt-in process is used. Recipients may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Information Collected

Inbound SMS replies are logged in a private Google Sheet accessible only to Christopher Smith. Logged data includes message content, sender phone number, and timestamp. This data is used solely to personalize future workout plans and is never shared, sold, or used for any commercial purpose.

Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with any third party for marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes. Message delivery is facilitated through Twilio (twilio.com) and Zapier (zapier.com) under their respective terms of service and privacy policies.

Data Retention

Message logs are retained indefinitely for personal wellness tracking purposes and may be deleted by the operator at any time upon request.

Your Rights

Recipients may request deletion of their message history or opt out of all future messages at any time by replying STOP or contacting chris@brandywineoaktax.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy may be directed to Christopher Smith at chris@brandywineoaktax.com.
